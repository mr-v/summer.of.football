Summer of Football

2014 Football Cup for Twitter - World Championship in Brazil - soccer, photos, live commentary and score, results
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Summer of Football adds even more entertainment to your World Championship experience. Enjoy beautiful photos and get a peek behind the scenes with Tweets posted by the players. During games follow commentary by the top experts and get more insight with statistics. In time between games read about the latest events. Get the best from the millions of Tweets while keeping your home timeline uncluttered. Join teams from around the world!

Gestures and Shortcuts

To scroll to the top of the current category tap its name. You can close a photo in three ways:

  • by tapping,
  • by swiping up if photo fits vertically on the screen,
  • by pinching-in.

Actions on a Tweet

After long press you can:

  • write a new Tweet,
  • post a reply to tweet author,
  • favorite a Tweet,
  • retweet. Other actions:
  • zoom in a photo,
  • open link in the in-app browser (then either add it to a Reading List or open in Safari).
